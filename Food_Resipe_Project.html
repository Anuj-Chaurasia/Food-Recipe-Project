Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Cottage Cheese (Milk) Solids
  • Wheat Semolina
  • Fortified Wheat Flour
  • Ghee

Recips

  1. Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup remove from fire; add cardamom and keep aside to cool.
  2. In a mixing bowl put the flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé MILKMAID,baking powder and baking soda.Mix gently to make a soft dough, do not knead too much.
  3. Divide the mixture into 30-35 portions and gently roll into round gulab jamuns. Fry in the oil at very low flame till golden brown in color.
  4. Keep putting the fried gulab jamuns in the prepared sugar syrup, once all the gulab jamuns are in the sugar syrup bring it to a boil and remove from flame.
  5. Serve warm.

Gughara

Ingredients

  • 2 Cup Maida
  • 4 Tbsp Melted Ghee
  • ½ cup Water or as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Recips

  1. Knead the dough for about 1 min.
  2. Divide it into equal portions and make smooth round balls.
  3. Using one dough ball roll it into a small Puri Shape.
  4. Place about 1-2 Tbsp of stuffing in the middle.
  5. Using a little water line the edges of the Puri and cover the mixture by joining one side with the other.
  6. Now take the Ghughra in your hand and lightly press the edges between your fingers & make pleats to give Ghughra Design. You can also make designs on ghughra using a fork.
  7. Repeat the process for the rest of Gujiyas.
  8. Heat Oil for Frying ghuhghra. Once the oil is hot reduce the flame to low and Fry 5-6 ghughra at a time.
  9. Fry till Ghughra till it becomes light golden colour. One batch takes around 5-7 min for frying.
  10. Once done take it out on a paper napkin.
  11. Allow Ghughra to cool down completely and then put it in an air-tight container.
